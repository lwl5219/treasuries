                            熟词生义

                            「train」：并不是大家熟悉的火车的意思噢，指的是长礼服的拖地部分，“裙裾”：the part of a long formal dress that spreads out on the floor behind the person wearing it.

                            原文：And the lords of the bedchamber took greater pains than ever, to appear holding up a train, although, in reality, there was no train to hold. 

                            e.g. The bride’s train was carried by her two young nephews.新娘的两个小外甥拖着裙尾。

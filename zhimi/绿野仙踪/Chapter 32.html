【0】 The next morning Dorothy kissed the pretty green girl good-bye, and they all shook hands with the soldier with the green whiskers, who had walked with them as far as the gate. When the Guardian of the Gate saw them again he wondered greatly that they could leave the beautiful City to get into new trouble. But he at once unlocked their spectacles, which he put back into the green box, and gave them many good wishes to carry with them.
第二天早晨，多萝茜吻别了绿衣女郎，他们一起和长着绿胡须的兵士握着手，他送他们一块儿到城门口。当守城门的人又看见了他们，十分吃惊，他们竟要离开这个美丽的城市，去进入新的困境。他立刻解下了他们的眼镜，放进绿箱子里去，并且祝他们好运。

    【1】 “You are now our ruler,” he said to the Scarecrow; “so you must come back to us as soon as possible.”
    　　“你是我们的新皇帝，”他对稻草人说，“所以你必须尽快地赶回到我们这里来。”

      【2】 “I certainly shall if I am able,” the Scarecrow replied; “but I must help Dorothy to get home, first.”
      　　“当然，如果我能够赶快，我会赶快的，”稻草人回答说，“但是，首先我必须帮助多萝茜回到家里去。”

        【3】 As Dorothy bade the good-natured Guardian a last farewell she said: “I have been very kindly treated in your lovely City, and everyone has been good to me. I cannot tell you how grateful I am.”
        　　至于多萝茜，她向着好耐性的守门人，作着最后一次的告别，她说： 　　“在你们这个可爱的城中，我受到很好的待遇，每一个人都很好地对待我。我无法告诉你我是多么的感激。”

The sun shone brightly as our friends turned their faces toward the Land of the South.
          　　“我的亲爱的朋友，别客气，”他回答说，“我们欢迎你和我们在一起，但是倘使你愿意回到堪萨斯州去，我希望你能够找到这条路。”于是他打开外城的大门，让他们走了出去，踏上旅途出发了。 　　当他们转过身来走向南方的国土去时，太阳很明亮地照耀着。

          【5】 They were all in the best of spirits, and laughed and chatted together. Dorothy was once more filled with the hope of getting home, and the Scarecrow and the Tin Woodman were glad to be of use to her. As for the Lion, he sniffed（吸） the fresh air with delight and whisked his tail from side to side in pure joy at being in the country again, while Toto ran around them and chased the moths and butterflies, barking merrily all the time.

            【6】 “City life does not agree with me at all,” remarked the Lion, as they walked along at a brisk（轻快的） pace. “I have lost much flesh since I lived there, and now I am anxious for a chance to show the other beasts how courageous I have grown.”
            　　“城市的生活，对于我是完全不适合的，”当他们活泼地、轻快地一步一步向前走过去时，狮子说，“自从我住在城里以来，瘦得很多了，现在我渴望有一个机会，向别的野兽们表现出我怎样地有胆量。”

            　　如今他们转过身来，向翡翠城看了最后的一眼，望见在绿色城墙的后面，有许多的城堞和礼拜堂上的尖顶，还有奥芝宫殿的螺旋形的圆屋顶，高出在一切东西。 　　“奥芝毕竟不是多么坏的一个魔术家。”正当铁皮人觉得他的心在他的胸膛里格格地发响时，他说。

                                        Chapter 32 内容精讲

                                        熟词生义

                                        「shoulder」：这个词大家都应该很熟悉，常作为名词，是“肩膀”的意思。在这里作动词，表示“扛，背”之意。

                                        原文："I believe I will try it myself," said the Woodman, and shouldering his axe, he marched up to the first tree that had handled the Scarecrow so roughly.

                                        e.g. She shouldered her bag and set off home. 她扛起包朝家走去。



                                        今日短语

                                        1. not agree with sb 使难受，不适合

                                        2. a dose of 一份

                                        3. bid (sb.) farewell 向某人道别



                                        句子解析

                                        1. There was no way of going around it, for it seemed to extend to the right and left as far as they could see; and, besides, they did not dare change the direction of their journey for fear of getting lost.

                                        「for fear of」：唯恐，以免。

                                        这句话的意思是：在密林四周，都没有好走的路，因为那树林仿佛从右到左，在他们所能看见的范围内一直伸展开去；而且，他们不敢改变他们旅程的方向，因为怕迷了路。


                                        2. So he walked forward to the tree, but just as he came under the first branches they bent down and twined around him, and the next minute he was raised from the ground and flung headlong among his fellow travelers.

                                        「bend down」：俯身

                                        「twine around」：缠绕

                                        「fling headlong」：头朝下地抛掷



                                        这句话主要描述了大树把稻草人缠起来扔出去的场景。看过哈利波特的读者们有没有联想到电影里面那棵打人柳呢？这里这棵捉人的树和打人柳十分相似，说不定罗琳就是从绿野仙踪里获得的创作灵感呢~

                                        这句话的意思是：所以他先走向前去，但是正当他走到最前面的树枝的底下面，它们都弯了下来，缠卷着他，接着，把他从地面上高高地举起来，并且把他倒栽葱式地抛向他同伴一行人。


                                        3. "I believe I will try it myself," said the Woodman, and shouldering his axe, he marched up to the first tree that had handled the Scarecrow so roughly. When a big branch bent down to seize him the Woodman chopped at it so fiercely that he cut it in two.

                                        「march up to」：向……走去

                                        「cut in two」：砍成两半

                                        这段话的意思是：“我相信我应该去试试看，”铁皮人说着，举起了他的斧头，向第一株树走去，就是刚才粗暴地缠着稻草人的那株树。当一枝大树枝弯下来捉他时，铁皮人凶猛地砍向它，直接把它劈成两段。


                                        4. At once the tree began shaking all its branches as if in pain, and the Tin Woodman passed safely under it.

                                        "Come on!" he shouted to the others. "Be quick!" They all ran forward and passed under the tree without injury, except Toto, who was caught by a small branch and shaken until he howled.

                                        But the Woodman promptly chopped off the branch and set the little dog free.

                                        大家在铁皮人的帮助下成功地过了这关，只不过Toto好可怜呀~

                                        「tree began shaking all its branches」：那棵树上所有的树枝都颤动起来

                                        「promptly chopped off the branch」：干脆利落地把树枝砍断

                                        「set the little dog free」：救出了这只小狗

                                        这几句话的意思是：那棵树上所有的树枝都颤动起来，仿佛很疼的样子。铁皮人安全地从树下走了过去。

                                        他对同伴们叫喊着，“过来！快一点儿！”

                                        他们一齐向前跑，毫发无伤地从树下穿了过去。只有托托被一枝小树捉住了，提溜在空中摇来晃去，吓得他汪汪乱叫。铁皮人干脆利落地把那根树枝砍断，救出了这只小狗。


                                        5. The other trees of the forest did nothing to keep them back, so they made up their minds that only the first row of trees could bend down their branches, and that probably these were the policemen of the forest, and given this wonderful power in order to keep strangers out of it.

                                        「keep strangers out of it」：把异乡人挡在森林外

                                        这句话的意思是：这个森林里的其他树木，却不曾做出类似动作来阻止他们，所以他们认定，只有第一排树木能够弯下它们的枝来捉人，它们或许是森林的警察，才有这样奇妙的本领，目的在于赶出陌生的客人。


                                        6. The four travelers walked along with ease through the trees until they came to the farther edge of the wood. Then, to their surprise, they found before them a high wall which seemed to be made of white china. It was smooth, like the surface of a dish, and higher than their heads.

                                        「made of white china」：用白瓷砖砌的

                                        「smooth, like the surface of a dish」：光滑得像瓷盆的平面

                                        这三句话的意思是：四个旅行者轻松地穿过了树林，最后来到森林边缘。这时，他们惊讶地发现，前面有一带高墙，比他们的头顶还高出一些，好像是用白瓷砖砌的，光滑得像瓷盆的平面。

                                        遇到了一堵墙，他们怎么过去？墙的后面又会是什么呢？

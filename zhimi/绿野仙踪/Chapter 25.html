                            熟词生义

                            1. 「handsome」：常见的意思是“潇洒的，英俊的”，比如a handsome man。在文中表示“雄伟的”，用来修饰建筑物。

                            原文：Her name was Gayelette, and she lived in a handsome palace built from great blocks of ruby.

                            e.g. Handsome Renaissance buildings.雄伟的文艺复兴时期建筑


                            2. 「spare」：常常作为形容词，表示“空闲的”，比如常说的spare time-业余时间。在文中以动词身份出现，意思是“宽恕，饶恕”

                            原文：…so that Gayelette finally spared them, on condition that the Winged Monkeys should ever after do three times the bidding of the owner of the Golden Cap.

                            e.g. They killed the men and spared the children. 他们杀害了男人，饶过了孩子。



                            今日短语

                            1. mate with 与…约会

                            2. be dressed in 穿着

                            3. be spoiled by 被宠坏

                            4. set eyes on 看见



                            句子解析

                            1."Once," began the leader, "we were a free people, living happily in the great forest, flying from tree to tree, eating nuts and fruit, and doing just as we pleased without calling anybody master.Perhaps some of us were rather too full of mischief at times, flying down to pull the tails of the animals that had no wings, chasing birds, and throwing nuts at the people who walked in the forest..."

                            “从前，”猴王开讲了，“我们是自由民，快乐地住在广袤的大森林里，在树木之间飞来飞去，每天吃坚果和水果，随心所欲，不必听从任何人的吩咐。也许，我们中间有些猴子太喜欢时不时地恶作剧了。他们会飞下去拽那些没有翅膀的动物们的尾巴，他们还追逐鸟儿，用坚果砸森林里的行人...”

                            「once」：从前，以前；曾经，一度

                            「call sb. master」：听从某人的吩咐

                            「mischief」：恶作剧，淘气，捣蛋

                            例如：Maybe a new bike would keep him out of mischief.也许一辆新自行车会让他不再淘气。

                            「at that time」：那时候，在那时，在当时

                            虽然都是称呼，但fellow与person不同，fellow是对人比较亲近的称呼，而person则是对一般人的统称哦~

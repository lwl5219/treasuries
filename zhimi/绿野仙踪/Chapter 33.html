                                                          Chapter 33 内容精讲

                                                          熟词生义

                                                          1. 「still」：这个词常作为副词频繁出现，表示“仍然”之意。在这里是形容词，表示“平静的”。

                                                          原文：Then Toto came up, and immediately began to bark, but Dorothy made him be still.

                                                          e.g. The kids found it hard to stay still. 孩子们觉得待着不动很难做到。


                                                          2. 「due」：常作为形容词以词组形式“due to”出现，表示“由于”，这在之前的章节中出现过哦~但在这里是比较生僻的用法，作为副词，表示“正向，正对着”。

                                                          原文："for it would be unwise for us to go any other way except due South."

                                                          e.g. to sail due east 向正东航行


                                                          3. 「fair」：这个词意思很丰富哦~作为形容词最为大家所熟悉的意思是“公正的”，在这里是另一个意思，表示“美丽的”。不知道大家有没有看过奥黛丽.赫本主演的一部电影《My Fair Lady》-《窈窕淑女》，就是这个单词了。

                                                          原文："My lady fair, Why do you stare at poor old Mr. Joker?

                                                          e.g. a fair maiden 美丽的少女



                                                          今日短语

                                                          1. a stretch of（尤指长而窄的）一片地域

                                                          2. pull sth. up 拉起

                                                          3. take pains to do 煞费苦心做某事

                                                          4. be vexed to 感到恼火

                                                          5. cast a glance (at sb.) （朝某人）投去一瞥



                                                          句子解析

                                                          1. After a time the ladder was finished. It looked clumsy, but the Tin Woodman was sure it was strong and would answer（满足） their purpose. The Scarecrow waked Dorothy and the Lion and Toto, and told them that the ladder was ready. The Scarecrow climbed up the ladder first, but he was so awkward that Dorothy had to follow close behind and keep him from falling off.

                                                          「answer their purpose」：满足他们的目的

                                                          「follow close behind」：紧紧地跟在后面

                                                          「keep him from falling off」：防止他掉下来

                                                          这段话的意思是：过了一会儿，梯子做成功了。看起来似乎很笨重，但是铁皮人确信它是坚固耐用的，并且能够达到他们的目的。稻草人唤醒了多萝茜，还有狮子和托托，告诉他们梯子已经做成了。

                                                          稻草人第一个爬上梯子去，但是他爬得这样地笨拙，使得多萝茜不得不紧紧地跟在后面，防止他掉下来。

                                                          梯子做好了，大家一起爬~想象一下这个场景，有没有觉得超级萌~


                                                          2.When they were all sitting in a row on the top of the wall, they looked down and saw a strange sight. Before them was a great stretch of country having a floor as smooth and shining and white as the bottom of a big platter. Scattered around were many houses made entirely of china and painted in the brightest colors.

                                                          现在，他们在墙头齐刷刷地坐成一排，向下面附望着那幅奇特的景象。他们眼前是好大一片土地，地面光滑、闪亮、洁白，就像一个大浅盘子的底。散落在各处的房屋都是瓷造的，漆着最鲜艳的颜色。

                                                          「in a row」：词组“一连，成一行”，在句中作状语。

                                                          「sight」：景象，视野

                                                          「a stretch of」：一片，例如：The place used to be a stretch of barren land. 早先这里是一片不毛之地。

                                                          「platter」：大浅盘（美语）


                                                          3.There were milkmaids and shepherdesses, with brightly colored bodices and golden spots all over their gowns; and princesses with most gorgeous frocks（长袍） of silver and gold and purple; and shepherds dressed in knee breeches（短裤） with pink and yellow and blue stripes down them, and golden buckles（搭扣） on their shoes; and princes with jeweled crowns upon their heads, wearing ermine robes and satin doublets; and funny clowns in ruffled gowns, with round red spots upon their cheeks and tall, pointed caps.   

                                                          「with brightly colored bodices and golden spots all over their gowns」：亮色有金黄色斑点的外衣。

                                                          「frock」：长袍

                                                          「knee breech」：短裤

                                                          「ermine robe」：白鼬皮的长袍

                                                          「satin doublet」：缎制的紧身衣

                                                          「ruffled gown」：皱边的长袍

                                                          这段话生词较多，因此较难理解，句型乍一看复杂但其实比较单一，都是用with引导的句子表示修饰~

                                                          译文：捋牛奶的女郎和牧羊的女郎，全都穿着亮色有金黄色斑点的外衣；公主们穿着最华丽的银色、金色和紫色的长袍；牧童穿着淡红色和黄色的短裤，垂着蓝色的绺条，在他们的鞋子上有黄色的钮扣；皇子们的头上，戴着用宝石装饰的皇冠，穿着白鼬皮的长袍和闪光缎的紧身衣；还有滑稽有趣的小丑们，穿着皱边的长袍，两边面颊上点着红的圆点，尖顶的高帽子覆盖到他们的额上。


                                                          4. But the pretty milkmaid was much too vexed to make any answer. She picked up the leg sulkily and led her cow away, the poor animal limping on three legs. As she left them the milkmaid cast many reproachful glances over her shoulder at the clumsy strangers, holding her nicked（受伤的） elbow close to her side.

                                                          「too vexed to make any answer」：气得没有理睬他们

                                                          「pick up the leg sulkily」：愤怒地拾起断腿

                                                          「cast many reproachful glances over her shoulder at the clumsy strangers」：好几次隔着肩膀投来责备的目光

                                                          这几句话的意思是：但是这个美丽的捋牛奶女郎太生气了，不理睬他们。她愤怒地拾起断腿，牵着牛走了。那可怜的畜生，只好三只脚跛行着。当那捋牛奶的女郎离开了他们，经过这些愚笨的陌生客人后，她回过头来，好几次隔着肩膀投来责备的目光，并且把受伤的臂肘紧紧夹在身侧。


                                                          5. Indeed, a jolly little clown came walking toward them, and Dorothy could see that in spite of his pretty clothes of red and yellow and green he was completely covered with cracks, running every which way and showing plainly that he had been mended in many places.

                                                          这段话就是对于小丑的外观描写~

                                                          「in spite of」：尽管

                                                          这几句话的意思是：真的，一个轻松愉快的小丑，现在向他们走来了，不管他红色、黄色和绿色的衣服怎样美丽，Dorothy能够看出他完全有了裂缝了，每跑一步路都能清楚地显示他有好多地方是修补过了。


                                                          6. "You see, here in our country we live contentedly, and can talk and move around as we please. But whenever any of us are taken away our joints at once stiffen, and we can only stand straight and look pretty. Of course that is all that is expected of us when we are on mantels（壁炉架） and cabinets（橱柜） and drawing-room tables, but our lives are much pleasanter here in our own country."

                                                          这段话是瓷公主面对Dorothy提出想把她带走时所说的~谁也不愿意供人赏玩，自由是每个灵魂都向往的。

                                                          「our joints at once stiffen」：我们的关节立刻变得僵硬了。

                                                          「mentel and cabinet」：壁炉架和橱柜

                                                          「drawing-room table」：客厅桌子

                                                          这几句话的意思是：“你要知道，这里是我们的国土，我们很心满意足地居住着，谈天或是四周行动都随着我们高兴。但是在我们中间不论哪个被拿走了，我们的关节立刻变得僵硬了，只能够笔直地站着，供人们赏玩罢了。当然，人们希望我们站在炉架上和橱柜里，还有站在客厅的桌子上，但是我们住在自己的国土里，才觉得更加快乐呢。”

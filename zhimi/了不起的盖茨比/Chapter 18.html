                                      The Great Gatsby  Chapter 18 内容精讲

                                      熟词生义

                                      1.「have」：have 在本文有特殊的意思，即：to give birth to sb/sth 生；生产。

                                      原文：The next April Daisy had her little girl and they went to France for a year.

                                      e.g. She's going to have a baby. 她快生孩子了。


                                      2.「fast」：不是“快的、迅速的”意思，在本文中作形容词，根据剑桥英语字典，它的意思是：without moral principles，放荡的。

                                      原文：They moved with a fast crowd, all of them young and rich and wild, but she came out with an absolutely perfect reputation.

                                      e.g. Life in Detroit no longer satisfied him; he wanted the fast life of California. 他不再满意底特律的生活，想要过加利福尼亚那种刺激快乐的生活。


                                      3.「time」：除了用作名词，time也可以用作动词，且在本文的意思是：to arrange to do sth or arrange for sth to happen at a particular time 为…安排时间；选择…的时机。

                                      原文：you can time any little irregularity of your own so that everybody else is so blind that they don't see or care.

                                      e.g. She timed her arrival for shortly after 3. 她定在 3 点钟刚过到达。


                                      4.「dip」：通常的意思是：“蘸、浸”，但在本文它的意思是：go downward，往下走。dip under a little bridge 指走到一座小桥下面。

                                      原文：It was dark now, and as we dipped under a little bridge I put my arm around Jordan's golden shoulder and drew her toward me and asked her to dinner.

                                      e.g. The sun dipped below the horizon. 太阳落下了地平线。


                                      5.「tough」：tough 不仅可以用作形容词，也可以用作名词，在本文中它的意思为：someone who often behaves in a violent way，暴徒，恶棍。

                                      原文：You see he's a regular tough underneath it all.

                                      e.g. Bands of armed toughs roamed the city. 几群持有武器的暴徒在这座城市里游荡。


                                      6.「dance」：看到这个单词第一反应“v. 跳舞”，但本章里它却是名词，意思是：a social event at which people dance 舞会。

                                      原文：It was that night he sent for me at his dance, and you should have heard the elaborate way he worked up to it.

                                      e.g. We hold a dance every year to raise money for charity. 我们每年举行一场舞会，为慈善事业募捐。


                                      7.「façade」：接触过façade这个词的人一般都知道它的意思有“假象、表面”，但在本章它的意思却是：the front of a building （建筑物的）正面，立面。

                                      原文：We passed a barrier of dark trees, and then the facade of Fifty-ninth Street, a block of delicate pale light, beamed down into the park.

                                      e.g. The gallery's elegant façade was built in the 18th-centur. 美术馆典雅的正面建于18世纪。

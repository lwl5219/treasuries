      这里是口语中出现了省音的状况，you'd of thought = you'd have thought = you would have thought.后者弱读时跟前者读音一样.作者为了表示说话的人是这样说的,就把句子写成了现在的样子.如果写成you would have thought或者you'd have thought读起来就不一样了,也就跟实际说话的效果不一样了.

                                                                                熟词生义

                                                                                1.「elaborate」: 这个最常用做动词，意为“详尽阐述；详细描述”，但是在本文中它是形容词，意思是：very complicated and detailed; carefully prepared and organized 复杂的；详尽的；精心制作的。

                                                                                原文: Mrs. Wilson had changed her costume some time before and was now attired in an elaborate afternoon dress of cream colored chiffon…

                                                                                e.g. She had prepared a very elaborate meal. 她做了一顿精美的饭菜。


                                                                                2.「regard」: regard最常见的意思是“认为”，但在本文里他的意思是：to look at sb/sth, especially in a particular way （尤指以某种方式）注视，凝视。

                                                                                原文：Mr. McKee regarded her intently with his head on one side and then moved his hand back and forth slowly in front of his face.

                                                                                e.g. He regarded us suspiciously. 他以怀疑的眼光看着我们。


                                                                                3.「present」: present最常用做前置形容词，意思是“当前的、现存的”，但是在本文他作为后置形容词使用，意思是：(of a person 人 ) being in a particular place 出现；在场；出席。

                                                                                原文：The bottle of whiskey—a second one—was now in constant demand by all present, excepting Catherine who “felt just as good on nothing at all."

                                                                                e.g. There were 200 people present at the meeting.有 200 人出席会议。


                                                                                4.「study」: study有“书房、学习、学业”的意思，可以用作动词和名词，在本文它的含义比较少见，但是学绘画的同学可要留意了（敲黑板！！！）study此处意思是：a drawing or painting of sth, especially one done for practice or before doing a larger picture 习作；试作；试画。

                                                                                原文：“Two studies. One of them I call ‘Montauk Point—the Gulls,' and the other I call ‘Montauk Point—the Sea.' "

                                                                                e.g. This is my nude study. 这是我的裸体画习作。

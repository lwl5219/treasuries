                                              A Study In Scarlet  Chapter 24 内容精讲


                                              熟词生义

                                              1.「knit」: 常见的意思是“编织”，在文中和brows搭配使用，表示“皱眉”。

                                              原文：Ferrier remained silent for some little time with his brows knitted. 

                                              e.g. They knitted their brows and started to grumble... 他们皱着眉头抱怨起来。


                                              2. 「will」：常作为动词，意思为“将会；决心要”，这个大家都知道啦。在文中作名词，意思是“意愿”：what sb wants to happen in a particular situation.

                                              原文：… that you should put your weak wills against the orders of the Holy Four!

                                              e.g. I was forced to sign the agreement against my will. 我被迫违心地签了这份协议。


                                              今日短语

                                                1. be scared of 害怕

                                              2. make no answer不答话

                                              3. deprive sb. of sth. 剥夺

                                              4. broach sth. to 向...提出

                                              5. somehow or other 设法

                                              6. run up against 撞上

                                              7. be afeared about 担心

                                              8. In the meantime, don't you fret yourself, my dearie, and don't get your eyes swelled up, else he'll be walking into me when he sees you. There's nothing to be afeared about, and there's no danger at all.

                                              「fret yourself」：让自己忧虑

                                              「swell up」：（眼睛）肿起

                                              「walk into」：痛骂，找麻烦

                                              「be afeared about」：害怕

                                              在这期间不要焦急，也不要把眼睛哭肿。否则他（Hope）看到你这样，就会来找我的麻烦。没有什么可怕的，根本不会有什么危险。

                                              Ferrier此时能做的也许就是安慰Lucy了吧～


                                              9. If he comes browsing（放牧）about this farm, he might chance to run up against a charge of buckshot travelling in the opposite direction.

                                              「run up against」：撞上

                                              「charge」：（装进炸弹或枪炮内的）炸药

                                              browse是“放牧”的意思，这句话字面上是说，摩门教的邪恶势力到Ferrier的庄园来放牧，其实意指他们在Ferrier的地盘上横行霸道。

                                              如果他要到这个农场横行霸道（comes browsing about this farm），就要尝尝迎面飞来的子弹（run up against a charge of buckshot travelling in the opposite direction）。


                                              10. John Ferrier uttered these consoling remarks in a very confident tone, but she could not help observing that he paid unusual care to the fastening of the doors that night, and that he carefully cleaned and loaded the rusty old shotgun which hung upon the wall of his bedroom. 

                                              「utter」：说出

                                              「consoling remarks」：安慰的话

                                              「in a confident tone」：以自信的语气

                                              「could not help doing」：忍不住做某事

                                              「fastening of the doors」：扣紧门

                                              「load the rusty old shotgun」：给生锈的旧猎枪装上子弹

                                              John Ferrier以非常自信的口气说出了这些安慰话，但当天夜里，她忍不住观察到，他非常谨慎地扣紧那些门，小心翼翼地擦亮挂在他卧室墙上那支生锈的旧猎枪，装上了子弹。

                                              1. 麦布女王（Queen Mab）

                                              “麦布女王”（Queen Mab）是一个神话故事中的仙女精灵，因莎翁的著名剧本“罗密欧与朱丽叶”中的相关描述而为世人熟知。根据剧本的描述，麦布女王可以帮助人类实现梦境。莎翁所描绘的麦布女王是个恶魔传播者，她在其所到之处都传播着病害。

                                              喏，据说她就长这副模样啦↓↓↓（是拖着裙子的那两个小精灵哦~）

                                              2. Puss-in-Boots（穿靴子的猫）

                                              《穿靴子的猫》（Puss-in-Boots）是法国作家夏尔·佩罗的作品。故事讲的是一只聪明的猫要帮助它穷困潦倒的主人翻身，于是它向主人要了一双靴子和一个布袋，穿过荆棘到森林里打猎。它把每次获得的猎物都献给了国王，它用机智和勇敢打败了富有的食人妖魔，最终它帮助主人得到了国王的青睐和公主的爱慕。 这部作品在2011年被美国梦工厂公司改编为动画电影。给大家欣赏一张萌照~


                                              熟词生义

                                              1. 「drawn」: 大家最为熟悉的一个意思就是’draw’的分词形式’drawn’,但是在这里drawn是作为形容词使用，表示“拔出的”。

                                              原文：and Peter kept watch outside with drawn sword.

                                                e.g.  She looked drawn and tired when she turned toward     me. 
                                                她转向我时，看上去既憔悴又疲惫。


                                              2. 「saw」：本意是指see的过去式，在这里是作动词，指“锯”

                                              原文：but every morning they sawed the trunk through.

                                                e.g.  He escaped by sawing through the bars of his cell.

                                                他锯断牢房的铁栅逃跑了。


                                              3. 「beam」: 这个词做名词的表示“横梁，电波”的意思，在这里做不及物动词，表示“堆满笑容”

                                              原文：Her face beamed when she exclaimed this.

                                              句子解析

                                              1. By and by she tucked them up in the great bed in the home under the trees, but she herself slept that night in the little house, and Peter kept watch outside with drawn sword, for the pirates could be heard carousing far away and the wolves were on the prowl.

                                              「by and by」: 不久

                                              「keep watch outside with drawn sword: keep watch是看守，值班的意思，这里是说Peter手持出鞘的刀在门口看守。

                                              「carousing far away」： 现在分词做伴随状语，海盗们正在远处狂欢作乐

                                              这句话是说：温迪在树下的屋子里，打发他们睡在大床上，给他们掖好被子；她自己那晚睡在小屋里。彼得手持出鞘的刀，不停地在外面巡逻，因为海盗们还在远处饮酒作乐，狼群也在四处觅食。

                                              3. Once you fitted, you drew in [let out] your breath at the top, and down you went at exactly the right speed, while to ascend you drew in and let out alternately, and so wriggled up.

                                              「draw in/let out one’s breath」: 吸气/呼气

                                              「ascend」: If something ascends, it moves up, usually vertically or into the air.通常来说是指垂直上升。例如：The road ascends steeply from the harbour. 这条路从港湾那里陡直而上。

                                              4. Once you fit, great care must be taken to go on fitting, and this, as Wendy was to discover to her delight, keeps a whole family in perfect condition.

                                              「great care must be taken」: 这里就是用了被动语态，短语就是：take great care to do sth, 做某事要格外小心

                                              「to her delight」: 令她开心的是，

                                              有的小可爱可能对and this, 之后的句子有点迷乱，其实as Wendy was to discover to her delight,是做插入语，后半句话的主句就是and this keeps a whole family in perfect condition. 而这里的this指的就是前一句话中所说的“要格外小心地保持这种状态”这件事。

                                              一旦合适了，就得格外小心，保持这种合适的状态。后来，温迪高兴地发现，正因为这，全家人才维持着良好的身体状况。


                                              5. There was an enormous fireplace which was in almost any part of the room where you cared to light it, and across this Wendy stretched strings, made of fibre, from which she suspended her washing.

                                              「care to do sth」: 愿意做某事/喜欢做某事。例如：She doesn’t care to spend much time with her relatives. 她不喜欢花很多时间跟亲戚们在一起。

                                              「stretch strings」: stretch在这里是指“拉紧，”

                                              「suspend」: 有“暂停；悬浮”的意思，在这里根据下文应该理解成“悬挂，晾（衣服）”的意思。

                                              「washing」: 指的就是“要洗的衣物”

                                              屋里有一个极大的壁炉，几乎占满了整个屋子的各个部分，你愿意在哪儿生火都行。温迪在炉前拴上许多用植物纤维搓成的绳子，她把洗净的衣裳晾在上面。

                                              8. The couch, as she always called it, was a genuine Queen Mab, with club legs; and she varied the bedspreads according to what fruit- blossom was in season. Her mirror was a Puss-in-Boots, of which there are now only three, unchipped, known to fairy dealers; the washstand was Pie-crust and reversible, the chest of drawers an authentic Charming the Sixth, and the carpet and rugs the best (the early) period of Margery and Robin. There was a chandelier from Tiddlywinks for the look of the thing, but of course she lit the residence herself.

                                              「club legs」: 这可不是“俱乐部腿”哦，这里club的用法大家可能比较陌生，是指我们打扑克牌时候的“梅花”，也就是说床脚是三叶草的形状

                                              「in season」: 应时的，当令

                                              「unchipped」：完整的

                                              「chandelier」： 枝形吊灯，喏，就长这样啦↓↓↓

                                              她的床——她总是管它叫卧榻，真正是麦布女王式的，有三叶草形的床脚。床罩随着不同季节的果树花更换。她的镜子是穿长筒靴的猫用的那种镜子，在仙子商贩的货架上，如今只剩下三面还没有打碎的。洗脸盆是馅饼壳式的，可以翻过来；抽屉柜是货真价实的迷人六世时代的，地毯是马杰里和罗宾极盛时代(早期)的产品。一盏用亮片装饰的大吊灯，只不过挂在那儿摆摆样子；当然，她用自己的光就可以照亮她的住处。


                                              9. You never exactly knew whether there would be a real meal or just a make-believe, it all depended upon Peter's whim: he could eat, really eat, if it was part of a game, but he could not stodge [cram down the food] just to feel stodgy [stuffed with food], which is what most children like better than anything else; the next best thing being to talk about it.

                                              至于大家到底是真的吃饭还是只是做个吃饭的样子，这就全凭Peter的心血来潮啦（it all depended upon Peter's whim）他也能吃，能真吃，如果这是游戏的一部分；可是，他不能为了填饱肚皮去吃（could not stodge just to feel stodgy），而别的孩子多半都喜欢这样做。其次，他还喜欢谈吃。


                                              10. Wendy's favorite time for sewing and darning was after they had all gone to bed. Then, as she expressed it, she had a breathing time for herself; and she occupied it in making new things for them, and putting double pieces on the knees, for they were all most frightfully hard on their knees.

                                              「sewing and darning」：缝缝补补

                                              「breathing time」：喘口气的时间

                                              「occupied it in doing sth」：这里的it指的就是breathing time使自己忙于做某事

                                              「double pieces」: 其实就是指在膝盖上缝“双层布”的意思

                                              「they were all most frightfully hard on their knees」：frightfully hard,是说“非常地厉害”，联系上下文不难猜到，一定是因为小孩子们太顽皮总是把膝盖磕破咯~

                                              「fling up」: 向上挥

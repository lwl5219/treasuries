                                                                                                                                              Cinderella

                                                                                                                                              这是Wendy荣升“妈妈”一职以来给大家讲得第一个故事，相信《灰姑娘》的故事大家已经能倒背如流啦~一起来欣赏下美美的Cinderella


                                                                                                                                              熟词生义

                                                                                                                                              1. 「call」: 这个单词除了“打电话”，还有“为你打call”（这里的call 是“呼喊，呐喊”的意思）。还有第三种含义，就是“拜访”。

                                                                                                                                              原文："I will call again in the evening," Slightly said;

                                                                                                                                              e.g. He decided to pay a call on Tommy Cummings. 他决定去拜访汤米·卡明斯。


                                                                                                                                              2. 「touch」: 这个单词大家一定不陌生啦，就是“触摸”，但是在这里做（可数）名词表示“修饰、润色”：a small detail that is added to sth in order to improve it or make it complete.

                                                                                                                                              原文：Peter strode up and down, ordering finishing touches.

                                                                                                                                              e.g. Emma was putting the finishing touches to the cake. 埃玛在对蛋糕进行最后的修饰。


                                                                                                                                              3. 「sole」: 一般是做形容词指“唯一的，仅有的”，在这里做名词表示“鞋底”也可以指“脚掌”。

                                                                                                                                              原文：They were very ashamed, but Tootles gave the sole of his shoe, and it made an excellent knocker.

                                                                                                                                              e.g. The soles of his feet were caked in mud. 他的脚掌上沾满了泥巴。

                                                                                                                                              4. come back to sb 记起

                                                                                                                                              5. break down 损坏，破坏

                                                                                                                                              句子解析

                                                                                                                                              1. The difference between him and the other boys at such a time was that they knew it was make-believe, while to him make-believe and true were exactly the same thing. This sometimes troubled them, as when they had to make-believe that they had had their dinners.

                                                                                                                                              「at such a time」: 这种时候

                                                                                                                                              「make-believe」: 虚假的

                                                                                                                                              「trouble」: 这个可以理解为“让某人感到为难”

                                                                                                                                              在这种时候，彼得和别的孩子不同的地方是，他们知道这是假装的，可是对他来说，假装的和真的就是一回事。这一点，常常使他们感到为难，比如说，有时候他们不得不假装已经吃过了饭。


                                                                                                                                              2. "I will call again in the evening," Slightly said; "give her beef tea out of a cup with a spout to it"; but after he had returned the hat to John he blew big breaths, which was his habit on escaping from a difficulty.

                                                                                                                                              「a cup with a spout to it」: 带着一个壶嘴的茶杯

                                                                                                                                              「blew big breath」: 长舒一口气

                                                                                                                                              这句话就是说：“今晚我还要再来，”斯莱特利说，“用一只带嘴的杯子喂她牛肉茶。”他把帽子还给约翰时，不由得深深地吐了一口气，那是他逃脱难关时的一种习惯。


                                                                                                                                              3. They gurgled with joy at this, for by the greatest good luck the branches they had brought were sticky with red sap, and all the ground was carpeted with moss.

                                                                                                                                              「gurgle with joy」: 开心的咯咯笑

                                                                                                                                              「be sticky with」: 粘满

                                                                                                                                              「be carpeted with」: 覆盖着

                                                                                                                                              4. Peter strode up and down, ordering finishing touches. Nothing escaped his eagle eyes.

                                                                                                                                              「stride up and down」: stride本身有“跨过”的含义，up and down前前后后，Peter在房子门前踱来踱去，吩咐进行最后的小修小整（ordering finishing touches）。

He knocked politely, and now the wood was as still as the children, not a sound to be heard except from Tinker Bell, who was watching from a branch and openly sneering.

                                                                                                                                              「as still as」: 和……一样静悄悄

                                                                                                                                              「openly sneering」：公开讥笑

                                                                                                                                              「not a sound to be heard except from Tinker Bell」：除了Tinker Bell，什么声音也没有。

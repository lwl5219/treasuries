    【1】 His garb of woe suggested that he had lost in one catastrophe every relation he had in the world, even to cousins by marriage twice removed. 英文中，当removed这个词被用于描述关系时，意味着两个人是隔代关系。Once removed指的就是有一代的差距，比如你妈妈的cousin，就是你的cousin once removed。那么不难理解，twice removed 意味着有两代的差距，比如你grandmother的cousin就是你的cousin twice removed。 所以，这里提到的twice removed可见是非常遥远的亲戚关系了，中文里我们可以说八杆子都打不着。毛姆对Stroeve很mean，极尽嘲讽之姿。 His plumpness（肥胖） and his red, fat cheeks made his mourning not a little incongruous（不协调的）.

                                                        Chapter 22 内容精讲


                                                        熟词生义

                                                        1. 「spare」：常作为形容词“多余的”或者动词“省出（时间）”，在文中的意思是“瘦的“：thin and usually quite tall

                                                        原文：His father was a spare old man, his hands gnarled after the work of a lifetime, silent and upright. 

                                                        e.g. She was thin and spare , with a sharply intelligent face. 她又高又瘦，一副精明过人的模样。


                                                        2. 「picture」：“图画，照片“的意思大家都非常熟悉啦，在文中的意思是“头脑中的情景“：a mental image.

                                                        原文：His thoughts dwelt among pictures of what might have been, and the safety of the life he had refused filled him with longing.

                                                        e.g. They have in their mind a picture of what an alcoholic should look like.他们心中有一个酒鬼应有的样子。


                                                        今日短语

                                                        1. make up one's mind 下定决心

                                                        2. weigh sb. down 使某人负担太重，压垮

                                                        3. tread in 踩着，跟随者（脚步）

                                                        4. rebel against 反对

                                                        5. have a knack for 在…方面有诀窍

                                                        6. glow with 发出亮光，洋溢着

                                                        7. the vast number of 大量的


                                                        句子解析

                                                        1. He was dressed in the deepest mourning, and on his bowler was a broad black band. He had even a black border to his handkerchief. 

                                                        「the deepest mourning」：mourning的意思是： sadness that you show and feel because sb has died，所以这里指的是最深切的悼念

                                                        Stroeve身着重丧，圆顶礼帽上系着一条宽宽的黑丝带，连手帕也镶着黑边。

                                                        Stroeve的着装尽显他的悲痛，失去的是挚爱之人呐～


                                                        2. His garb of woe suggested that he had lost in one catastrophe every relation he had in the world, even to cousins by marriage twice removed. 

                                                        他这身悲痛的装束会让人以为，在一此灾祸中，他失去了世间所有的亲戚（lost every relation），甚至嫡表远亲。

                                                        英文中，当removed这个词被用于描述关系时，意味着两个人是隔代关系。Once removed指的就是有一代的差距，比如你妈妈的cousin，就是你的cousin once removed。那么不难理解，twice removed 意味着有两代的差距，比如你grandmother的cousin就是你的cousin twice removed。

                                                        所以，这里提到的twice removed可见是非常遥远的亲戚关系了，中文里我们可以说八杆子都打不着。毛姆对Stroeve很mean，极尽嘲讽之姿。


                                                        3. The ridicule he had endured for years seemed now to weigh him down, and the final blow of Blanche's treachery had robbed him of the resiliency which had made him take it so gaily. He could no longer laugh with those who laughed at him. He was an outcast. 

                                                        「weigh down」：If you are weighed down by something, it makes you extremely worried or causes you great problems. 压垮，使…承受压力，举个例子：Suffering weighs me down. 苦难压垮了我。

                                                        「rob of」：抢夺（某人），可以指抢劫实物，比如rob me of 100 dollars；也可以指抢夺虚拟的东西，比如rob me of my world. 剥夺了我的世界。文中说的是妻子的背叛剥夺了他笑对别人嘲笑（的能力）。Resiliency指的是快速恢复的能力。

                                                        「outcast」：a person who is not accepted by other people，被抛弃者

                                                        多少年来，Stroeve忍受的嘲笑，现在似乎已将他击垮（weigh him down），Blanche的背叛（treachery）给了他最后一击（the final blow），让他不再有能力笑着面对冷嘲热讽。他不再和那些嘲笑他的人一起大笑，他是一个被遗弃的人。


                                                        4. Cleanliness, indeed, was a mania with her. I saw a neat little old woman, with cheeks like apples, toiling away from morning to night, through the long years, to keep her house trim and spruce. 

                                                        这几句描述了Stroeve的母亲

                                                        「mania」：an extremely strong desire or enthusiasm for sth，狂热，极大的热情，他母亲很爱干净

                                                        「toil away」：长期劳累，这里说的是他母亲从早忙到晚，一刻不得闲，是踏实的人家呢

                                                        「trim and spruce」：这两个单词都表示整洁的，洁净的

                                                        洁净，的的确确已经成了她的癖好（mania）。我仿佛看到一个清爽利落的小老太太，长着苹果一样的面颊。日复一日从早忙到晚，把屋里屋外收拾得整洁利索。


                                                        5. Nothing ever happened in that little town, left behind by the advance of civilisation, and one year followed the next till death came, like a friend, to give rest to those who had laboured so diligently. 

                                                        这几句描述了Stroeve生活的小镇，意思不难理解～

                                                        文明日新月异，这座小城却被远远地抛在后面，好像永远也不会有什么事发生。如此年复一年，直到死神来临，像个失散多年的老朋友，让这些勤劳一生的人，永远长眠。


                                                        6. My father wished me to become a carpenter like himself. For five generations we've carried on the same trade, from father to son. Perhaps that is the wisdom of life, to tread in your father's steps, and look neither to the right nor to the left. 

                                                        「carry on」：If you carry on doing something, you continue to do it.继续下去，Stroeve本应继承父业，成为一个木匠。

                                                        「tread in your father's steps」：踩着父亲走过的脚步，也就是上面说的子承父业啦

                                                        我父亲希望我像他一样，做个木匠，我们家五代人都干这个行业，总是父一代子一代传承下去。也许这就是生活的智慧，永远踩着父亲的脚步走下去，既不左顾也不右盼。


                                                        7. When I was a little boy I said I would marry the daughter of the harness-maker（做马具的人） who lived next door. She was a little girl with blue eyes and a flaxen pigtail（亚麻色辫子）. She would have kept my house like a new pin, and I should have had a son to carry on the business after me.

                                                        「harness-maker」：做马具的人 

                                                        「like a new pin」：extremly clean, 来源于短语（as ） clean as a (new )

                                                        小的时候我对别人说我要同隔壁一家做马具人家的女儿结婚。她是一个蓝眼睛的小女孩，亚麻色的头发上梳一根小辫。要是同这个人结了婚，她会把我家收拾的井井有条，还会给我生个孩子接替我的行业。

                                                        这几句都描述了Stroeve本该过的生活，如果他没去意大利学画画，就会在荷兰老家子承父业跟隔壁家的女儿结婚生子…可是啊，人生没有如果呢！


                                                        8. His thoughts dwelt among pictures of what might have been, and the safety of the life he had refused filled him with longing. 

                                                        Stroeve的思绪萦绕在（dwell among）可能发生的幻景上，他现在渴望（with longing）他从前放弃的安稳生活。

                                                        「dwell」：的意思大家比较熟悉的是“居住”，比如dwell in the forest。此外，也有“沉溺于”的意义，英文释义是think moodily about something，比如：I’d rather not dwell on the past.我不想再沉溺于过去了。

                                                        子承父业呆在父母身边过安稳的日子，或者到大城市去追逐自己心中的那团火？看来不管何时，这都是年轻人需要面临的抉择，没有人知道作出选择之后会面临怎样的境地，但不要等覆水难收才想回头，毕竟这不是生或者死的问题。


                                                        9. The world is hard and cruel. We are here none knows why, and we go none knows whither. We must be very humble. We must see the beauty of quietness. We must go through life so inconspicuously that Fate does not notice us. And let us seek the love of simple, ignorant people. Their ignorance is better than all our knowledge. Let us be silent, content in our little corner, meek and gentle like them. That is the wisdom of life.

                                                        这是知米妞很喜欢的一段，不难理解，想要分享给你们。

                                                        世界是无情的，残酷的，我们生到人世间没有人知道为了什么，我们死后没有人知道到何处去，我们必须自甘卑微，我们必须看到冷清寂寥的美妙。在生活中，我们一定不要出风头，露头角，引起命运对我们的关注。让我们去寻求那些淳朴敦厚的人的爱情吧。他们的愚昧远比我们的知识更为可贵，让我们保持着沉默，满足于我们的小小天地，像他们一样平易温顺吧，这就是生活的智慧。


                                                        10. To me it was his broken spirit that expressed itself, and I rebelled against his renunciation. But I kept my own counsel. 

                                                        「rebel against」：反对，反抗

                                                        「keep my own counsel」：将自己的意见保密，保留自己的观点

                                                        对我来说，这些话是Stroeve破碎灵魂的自白，我反对（rebel against）他的自暴自弃。但我不想与他争辩（keep my own counsel）。


                                                        11. He glowed with happy pride. I thought of those cold scenes of his, with their picturesque peasants and cypresses and olive-trees. They must look queer in their garish frames on the walls of the peasant house.

                                                        「glow with」：发出亮光，洋溢着

                                                        「cypresses」；柏树，大家还记得前面Stroeve经常画的那些画吗？

                                                        Stroeve的脸上洋溢着（glow with）快乐和骄傲的光芒。我又响起他画的那些毫无感觉的场景，什么衣着鲜艳的（picturesque）农夫，柏树，橄榄树。这些画镶着颇为讲究的金边（garish frames），挂在农家的墙面上，真是格格不入啊（look queer）。


                                                        12. I hoped that the grief which now seemed intolerable would be softened by the lapse of time, and a merciful forgetfulness would help him to take up once more the burden of life. 

                                                        「lapse of time」：一段时间，时间的流逝

                                                        「take up」：f you take up something such as a task or a story, you begin doing it after it has been interrupted or after someone else has begun it. 继续;把…接着进行下去

                                                        我希望现在他无法忍受的（intolerable）悲伤，随着时间的推移（by the lapse of time），能慢慢减轻，仁慈的遗忘也能再次帮他振作起来（take up）。


                                                        13. He was young still, and in a few years he would look back on all his misery with a sadness in which there would be something not unpleasurable.

                                                        他还年轻，多年以后，当他回首（look back on）往日的悲痛遭遇，并非都是创伤（something not unpleasurable）。

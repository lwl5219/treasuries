野性的呼唤 Chapter 6

【0】 Three more huskies were added to the team inside an hour, making a total of nine, and before another quarter of an hour had passed they were in harness and swinging up the trail toward the Dyea Canon. Buck was glad to be gone, and though the work was hard he found he did not particularly despise it. He was surprised at the eagerness which animated the whole team and which was communicated to him; but still more surprising was the change wrought in Dave and Sol-leks. 「eagerness」：迫切之情 「be communicated to」：传染给…… 「wrought in」：使……发生了（变化），它的原型是“work”，“wrought”是旧时的过去分词形式，现在是“worked”。 在这里巴克的心态发生了变化，纵使每天的工作很辛苦，但是重新踏上征途的时候的心情是兴奋急切的，不仅仅是巴克，整个雪橇犬队伍都被这种激动的情绪感染了。 “which animated the whole team and which was communicated to him”为两个并列的定语从句修饰“eagerness”，表现这种临行前的急迫之情。“still more surprising was the change”为倒装句，起强调作用；“wrought in Dave and Sol-leks”为后置定语修饰change，其中“wrought”为“work”的过去式，这是旧时的用法，“wrought in”表示发生在……
不出一小时，这支队伍就又增加了三条爱斯基摩狗，使总数达到了九条。又过了不到两分钟，他们就给套上了缰绳，上了通往迪亚峡谷的雪道。巴克很欢喜离开这儿，虽说工作苦了点儿，但他并不感到特别的讨厌。让他惊讶的是一种急不可待的心情，全队被这种心情刺激了，也感染了他自己。然而使他更为惊讶的是戴夫和索尔雷克斯身上发生的变化。

    【1】 They were new dogs, utterly transformed by the harness. All passiveness and unconcern had dropped from them. They were alert and active, anxious that the work should go well, and fiercely irritable with whatever, by delay or confusion, retarded that work. 「fiercely irritable」：狂躁易怒的 「retard」：延误；减缓；使放慢速度 「by delay or confusion」：由于耽搁延误或者手忙脚乱；“by”表示原因。 这里讲的是戴夫和索尔雷克斯，他们是两只新来的成员，本来消极淡漠的他们一戴上挽具就彻底变成了热情四射，严以律己的劳模了。不仅是对自己，他们还严厉督促整个团队。 The toil of the traces seemed the supreme expression of their being, and all that they lived for and the only thing in which they took delight.
    这两条狗是新来的，挽具使他俩彻底变了样。消失了所有的消极和淡漠。他俩变得机警而活跃，一心要使工作进展顺利，不管耽搁还是乱阵，只要工作被延误，他俩就大发雷霆。他们生存的最高体现，他们活着为了是，他们惟一乐在其中的东西好像就是拉橇。

      【2】 Dave was wheeler or sled dog, pulling in front of him was Buck, then came Sol-leks; the rest of the team was strung out ahead, single file, to the leader, which position was filled by Spitz. Buck had been purposely placed between Dave and Sol-leks so that he might receive instruction. 「be purposely placed」：被有意安排 「receive instruction」：接受指点；得到指导 巴克的潜力是主人看在眼里的，它被特意安排在两个“严师”之间，接受魔鬼式的锻炼。 句子用了“so that”的句型阐明了它被安排的原因。 Apt scholar that he was, they were equally apt teachers, never allowing him to linger long in error, and enforcing their teaching with their sharp teeth. 「linger long in error」：持续错误的做法，这里表示不断重复错误的事情，没有纠正过来。 学习在这里是迫切而残酷的，否则将会得到利齿的教训。 「apt scholar that he was」：倒装句，这种类型的倒装句自带让步意味，相当于：though he was an apt scholar, 表示虽然巴克善于学习，但是他的两位老师也资质不差，教学有法。
      戴夫是驾辕狗，或者叫驾橇狗，巴克在他前面拉套，再往前是索尔雷克斯，其他狗在远远的前头跑着，排成一溜儿套在领头狗的身后，领头狗的职务则由斯匹次充当。戴夫和索尔雷克斯中间特意安排的是巴克，这样便于他得到指点。他对学习善长，而他俩也一样善于施教，有错必纠，而且用利齿来实施他们的训导。

        【3】 Dave was fair and very wise. He never nipped Buck without cause, and he never failed to nip him when he stood in need of it. As Francois's whip backed him up, Buck found it to be cheaper to mend his ways than to retaliate. Once, during a brief halt, when he got tangled in the traces and delayed the start, both Dave and Solleks flew at him and administered a sound trouncing.
        戴夫公正而明智，决不会毫无道理地咬他，而当他需要教训时，又不会少咬一口。由于戴夫有弗朗索瓦的鞭子撑腰，巴克发现改正错误要比报复更划得来。有一次小憩，他被缰绳缠住，耽搁了出发，戴夫和索尔雷克斯一齐冲上来，狠狠地教训了他一顿。

          【4】 The resulting tangle was even worse, but Buck took good care to keep the traces clear thereafter; and ere the day was done, so well had he mastered his work, his mates about ceased nagging him. Francois's whip snapped less frequently, and Perrault even honored Buck by lifting up his feet and carefully examining them.
          结果把缰绳搞得更乱了。但是从而以后巴克便很小心，不再把缰绳搞乱了。这一天还没到时间，巴克就能进退自如，不用两个同伴再咬他了。弗朗索瓦的鞭子响得少了，佩罗甚至优待了巴克，抬起他的脚，仔细地一只只检查了一遍。

            【5】 It was a hard day's run, up the Canon, through Sheep Camp, past the Scales and the timber line, across glaciers and snowdrifts hundreds of feet deep, and over the great Chilcoot Divide（奇尔库特分水岭）, which stands between the salt water and the fresh and guards forbiddingly the sad and lonely North.
            他们整整跑了一天，爬上峡谷，穿过羊寨，经过木材站，越过森林分界线，跨过一道道深达百尺的冰川和流动雪堆，而且还翻过了高耸的奇尔库特分水岭。它矗立在咸水与淡水之间，庄严地捍卫着凄凉而荒僻的北方。

              【6】 They made good time down the chain of lakes which fills the craters of extinct volcanoes, and late that night pulled into the huge camp at the head of Lake Bennett（本尼特湖）, where thousands of goldseekers were building boats against the break-up of the ice in the spring. Buck made his hole in the snow and slept the sleep of the exhausted just, but all too early was routed out in the cold darkness and harnessed with his mates to the sled.
              他们快马加鞭飞奔一路，经过一连串火山湖，深夜时分赶到了本尼特湖口的大营地。这里有成千上万的淘金者在造小船，春天冰融雪化的时候就可以使用了。巴克在雪地里为自己挖了一个洞，入睡时是一身疲惫，但主人在天还没蒙蒙亮，天气还冷森森的时候就把他早早地赶了出来，给他和伙伴们一起套上了雪橇。

                【7】 That day they made forty miles, the trail being packed; but the next day, and for many days to follow, they broke their own trail, worked harder, and made poorer time. 「the trail being packed」：这是一个独立主格的结构，依附于第一句主句，由于他们现在位于冰天雪地的北国，因此“being packed”其实就是压得做做实实的（雪道）。 「many days to follow」：接下来的几天，类似的表达例如：many years to come：接下来的几年。 「break the trail」：开道；大雪掩埋了路径，他们得自己找路走。 As a rule, Perrault travelled ahead of the team, packing the snow with webbed shoes to make it easier for them.
                由于一路上都是压得做做实实地雪道，这一天他们又跑了四十英里。但再过一天，还有以后的一些日子 ，他们就得自己开道，花的力气越来越大，跑的路却越来越少了。佩罗一如既往，脚蹬一双带蹼的靴子走在队伍的最前面，把雪道踩结实，这样狗跑起来就会省点力气。

                【8】 Francois, guiding the sled at the gee-pole（橇的舵棍）, sometimes exchanged places with him, but not often. Perrault was in a hurry, and he prided himself on his knowledge of ice, which knowledge was indispensable, for the fall ice was very thin, and where there was swift water, there was no ice at all.
                弗朗索瓦则驾驭着雪橇，有时候也和佩罗轮换一下，但不常换。佩罗着急赶路，对自己了解冰雪知识很是自豪。这种了解是必不可少的，因为秋天的冰很薄，而且在水流湍急的地方，根本就没有冰。

                  【9】 Day after day, for days unending, Buck toiled in the traces. Always, they broke camp in the dark, and the first gray of dawn found them hitting the trail with fresh miles reeled off behind them. 「break camp」：这个是很新鲜的说法，表示收帐篷，整理营地准备启程。 「fresh miles」：这里的“fresh”是“新鲜的”延伸意，表示刚刚的，说明他们速度很快，不一会就可以赶数英里的路。 「reeled off...」：过去分词做后置定语，修饰miles。从纺车上把线拉出，这是一个很形象的描写，道路如同轴上的丝线，一下子就被扯出来了，表示他们赶路速度很快。 「the first gray of dawn found them…」：我们经常能在英语中看到这种主语是非人，而人作为宾语的表达。该句子意为：天还没亮，他们就…… And always they pitched camp after dark, eating their bit of fish, and crawling to sleep into the snow. Buck was ravenous.
                  巴克套着缰绳作苦工，日复一日，没完没了。他们总是天不亮就拔营，当天边出现第一道微光时，数英里的路程早已被他们甩在了身后。他们宿营总是等天黑后，吃上几口鱼，然后钻进雪里睡觉。

                  【10】 The pound and a half of sun-dried salmon, which was his ration for each day, seemed to go nowhere. He never had enough, and suffered from perpetual hunger pangs.                     【11】 He swiftly lost the fastidiousness which had characterized his old life.                     【12】 He watched and learned.                         【16】 He did not steal for joy of it, but because of the clamor of his stomach. He did not rob openly, but stole secretly and cunningly, out of respect for club and fang. In short, the things he did were done because it was easier to do them than not to do them.

                          The Call of the Wild

                          Chapter 6 内容精讲

                          熟词生义

                          1. 「file」：我们知道“file”有“文件”的意思，在本文中它表示“排成一行的人（或物）”，英文释义：a line of people or things, one behind the other。

                          原文：…then came Sol-leks; the rest of the team was strung out ahead, single file, to the leader, which position was filled by Spitz.

                          e.g. They walked in single file. 他们排成一队走。


                          2. 「odds」：“odd”本身有“几率；奇数”等含义，文中它以复数的形式存在，表示“不利条件；掣肘的事情；逆境”，英文释义：something that makes it seem impossible to do or achieve sth。

                          原文：All his days, no matter what the odds, he had never run from a fight.

                          e.g. Against the odds, we will finally achieve success. 克服了种种苦难，我们终究会成功。


                          今日短语

                          1. be communicated to 传染给……

                          2. string out拉长；排列开来，排开

                          3. rout out驱逐；挖去；唤起

                          4. toil in sth. 跋涉；（长时间）苦干

                          5. in good condition情况良好；健康

                          6. rob sb. of sth. 偷窃某人的东西


                          句子解析

                          1. He was surprised at the eagerness which animated the whole team and which was communicated to him; but still more surprising was the change wrought in Dave and Sol-leks.

                          「eagerness」：迫切之情

                          「be communicated to」：传染给……

                          「wrought in」：使……发生了（变化），它的原型是“work”，“wrought”是旧时的过去分词形式，现在是“worked”。

                          “which animated the whole team and which was communicated to him”为两个并列的定语从句修饰“eagerness”，表现这种临行前的急迫之情。“still more surprising was the change”为倒装句，起强调作用；“wrought in Dave and Sol-leks”为后置定语修饰change，其中“wrought”为“work”的过去式，这是旧时的用法，“wrought in”表示发生在……

                          在这里巴克的心态发生了变化，纵使每天的工作很辛苦，但是重新踏上征途的时候的心情是兴奋急切的，不仅仅是巴克，整个雪橇犬队伍都被这种激动的情绪感染了。

                          句子大意：让他惊讶的是一种急不可待的心情，全队被这种心情刺激了，也感染了他自己。然而使他更为惊讶的是戴夫和索尔雷克斯身上发生的变化。


                          2. They were alert and active, anxious that the work should go well, and fiercely irritable with whatever, by delay or confusion, retarded that work.

                          「fiercely irritable」：狂躁易怒的

                          「retard」：延误；减缓；使放慢速度

                          「by delay or confusion」：由于耽搁延误或者手忙脚乱；“by”表示原因。

                          这里讲的是戴夫和索尔雷克斯，他们是两只新来的成员，本来消极淡漠的他们一戴上挽具就彻底变成了热情四射，严以律己的劳模了。不仅是对自己，他们还严厉督促整个团队。

                          句子大意：他俩变得机警而活跃，一心要使工作进展顺利，不管耽搁还是乱阵，只要工作延误，他俩就大发雷霆。


                          3. Buck had been purposely placed between Dave and Sol-leks so that he might receive instruction.

                          「be purposely placed」：被有意安排

                          「receive instruction」：接受指点；得到指导

                          巴克的潜力是主人看在眼里的，它被特意安排在两个“严师”之间，接受魔鬼式的锻炼。句子用了“so that”的句型阐明了它被安排的原因。

                          句子大意：戴夫和索尔雷克斯中间特意安排的是巴克，这样便于他得到指点。


                          4. Apt scholar that he was, they were equally apt teachers, never allowing him to linger long in error, and enforcing their teaching with their sharp teeth.

                          「apt scholar that he was」：倒装句，这种类型的倒装句自带让步意味，相当于：though he was an apt scholar, 表示虽然巴克善于学习，但是他的两位老师也资质不差，教学有法。

                          「linger long in error」：持续错误的做法，这里表示不断重复错误的事情，没有纠正过来。

                          学习在这里是迫切而残酷的，否则将会得到利齿的教训。

                          句子大意：他对学习善长，而他俩也一样善于施教，有错必纠，而且用利齿来实施他们的训导。


                          5. That day they made forty miles, the trail being packed; but the next day, and for many days to follow, they broke their own trail, worked harder, and made poorer time.

                          「the trail being packed」：这是一个独立主格的结构，依附于第一句主句，由于他们现在位于冰天雪地的北国，因此“being packed”其实就是压得做做实实的（雪道）。

                          「many days to follow」：接下来的几天，类似的表达例如：many years to come：接下来的几年。

                          「break the trail」：开道；大雪掩埋了路径，他们得自己找路走。

                          句子大意：由于一路上都是压得做做实实的雪道，这一天他们又跑了四十英里。但再过一天，还有以后的一些日子，他们就得自己开道，花的力气越来越大，跑的路却越来越少了。


                          6. Always, they broke camp in the dark, and the first gray of dawn found them hitting the trail with fresh miles reeled off behind them.

                          「break camp」：这个是很新鲜的说法，表示收帐篷，整理营地准备启程。

                          「the first gray of dawn found them…」：我们经常能在英语中看到这种主语是非人，而人作为宾语的表达。该句子意为：天还没亮，他们就……

                          「fresh miles」：这里的“fresh”是“新鲜的”延伸意，表示刚刚的，说明他们速度很快，不一会就可以赶数英里的路。

                          「reeled off...」：过去分词做后置定语，修饰miles。从纺车上把线拉出，这是一个很形象的描写，道路如同轴上的丝线，一下子就被扯出来了，表示他们赶路速度很快。

                          句子大意：他们总是天不亮就拔营，当天边出现第一道微光时，数英里的路程早已被他们甩在了身后。


                          7. A dainty eater, he found that his mates, finishing first, robbed him of his unfinished ration. There was no defending it. While he was fighting off two or three, it was disappearing down the throats of the others.

                          「a dainty eater」：饮食讲究的人，这里指的就是巴克

                          「there was no defending it」：难以防范；防不胜防

                          食物是在这荒野之中赖以生存的重要条件，但是每天极少的量让每一顿饭都成为一场战斗。像巴克这样一个曾处于上流社会的“dainty eater”在这里是注定要饿肚子的。

                          句子大意：他吃东西十分挑剔，结果他却发现他的伙伴们早早就吃完，然后他没吃完的口粮便给抢跑了，防不胜防，在他驱赶这几条狗的时候，东西就被另外的狗吞下去了。


                          8. It was all well enough in the Southland, under the law of love and fellowship, to respect private property and personal feelings; but in the Northland, under the law of club and fang, whoso took such things into account was a fool, and in so far as he observed them he would fail to prosper.

                          「under the law of love and fellowship」：介词短语补充说明“南国是以博爱和友情为准则的”。

                          「under the law of club and fang」：这里的“club(棍棒)”和“fang(利齿)”指代的就是冷酷无情的暴力和厮杀。

                          「whoso」：不管是谁；无论是谁

                          「in so far as」：到…的程度；只要

                          这里的世界不同于南边，北边的世界更趋向于原始状态，生命为了存活而斗争，而不是追求理想化的价值。

                          句子大意：尊重私有财产和他人感情，在以博爱和友情为准则的南国，那是再好不过的，但在以棍棒和利齿为法则的北国，谁要是相信这些东西，那他准是个呆子，要是抱住这些东西不放，那他就是个傻子。

                                                    熟词生义

                                                    1. 「excite」：提到excite，大家就会想到跟“激动”有关，但其实它还有“激发、引起”的意思噢：to make sb feel a particular emotion or react in a particular way，比如文中的excite kindness 引起善意

                                                    原文：for all the kindness ／which her beauty might otherwise have excited was obliterated in the minds of the spectators by the imagination of the enormity（弥天大罪） she was supposed to have committed.

                                                    e.g. to excite attention/criticism/ curiosity 

                                                    引起注意／批评／好奇心


                                                    2. 「produce」：常见的意思是“生产、产生”，在文中指的是“出示，使出现”：to show sth or make sth appear from somewhere.

                                                    原文：The picture was then produced which the servant had found in her pocket;

                                                    e.g. He produced a letter from his pocket. 

                                                    他从口袋拿出一封信。


                                                    3. 「bear」：不是“熊”也不是“承受”，而是“显示，带有”：to show sth

                                                    原文：and I hope the character I have always borne will incline my judges to a favourable interpretation where any circumstance appears doubtful or suspicious.

                                                    e.g. The title of the essay bore little relation to the contents. 这篇文章的标题与内容很不符。


                                                    4. 「knowledge」：“知识”的意思相信每一位小可爱都知道啦，在这里的意思是“知道，了解”：the state of knowing about a particular fact or situation. 短语“come to sb’s knowledge”则表示被某人知道

                                                    原文： If she had gone near the spot where his body lay, it was without her knowledge.

                                                    e.g. She sent the letter without my knowledge. 她背着我把信寄出去了。

                                                    2. for one’s defense 为…辩护

                                                    3. be well acquainted with 与...非常熟悉

                                                    5. That she had been bewildered when questioned by the market-woman ／was not surprising, since she had passed a sleepless night and the fate of poor William was yet uncertain.

                                                    「That…market-woman」：that引导的从句作为整个句子的主语，就是咱们说的主语从句啦，这时候谓语动词要用到单数形式，其实主干也就是Sth. was not suprising. 即当被市场的农妇询问的时候，Justine显得迷迷糊糊这件事也就不足为奇了。

                                                    「since」：因为，由于，后面解释迷糊的原因

                                                    大意是：而那个市场的农妇询问Justine的时候，就算她显得迷迷糊糊也没什么奇怪的，因为她一夜都没合眼，而且可怜的 William 当时还下落不明。


                                                    6. Elizabeth saw even this last resource, her excellent dispositions and irreproachable conduct, about to fail the accused, when, although violently agitated, she desired permission to address the court.

                                                    「her excellent dispositions」：她（Justine）的优秀性情；「irreproachable conduct」：无可指责的举止，and连接的这两个短语，具体解释前面的this last resources——这最后的办法

                                                    「the accused」：被控告的人，也就是Justine

                                                    「agitated」：showing in your behaviour that you are anxious and nervous，焦急不安的， Elizabeth很是着急

                                                    「address the court」：address在此处是“向...说话”的意思，也是比较常见的， Elizabeth决定亲自为Justine辩护了，大家会怎么想呢！

                                                    大意是：Elizabeth 已经意识到，被告最后一线希望——她的高尚品质和无可指责的为人——也起不了什么作用了，不禁焦急万分，于是她请求法庭准许她发言。


                                                    7. She had no temptation for such an action; as to the bauble（首饰） ／on which the chief proof rests, if she had earnestly desired it, I should have willingly given it to her, so much do I esteem and value her.

                                                    「 temptation」：这个单词也出现好几次啦，引诱，诱惑：the desire to do or have sth that you know is bad or wrong. Elizabeth认为Justine不会受到诱惑做这件事

                                                    「on which…」：从句部分，修饰前面的bauble，画像是主要的罪证

                                                    「so much do I esteem and value her」：so much 置于句首，表示强调，后面用了部分倒装（do I），意思等同于I do esteem and value her so much!

                                                    大意是：Justine不会鬼迷心窍到做出这样的事。至于那件认为是罪证的肖像，如果她真的那么想要，我是会非常乐意送给她的。我真的非常尊重她，信赖她。
